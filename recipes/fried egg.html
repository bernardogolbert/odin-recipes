Fried Egg Recipe

Home

Description

This is a delicious fried egg recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  2. Crack the egg into the skillet.
  3. Cook until the whites are set but the yolk is still runny, about 2-3 minutes. For a firmer yolk, cook an additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Carefully remove the egg from the skillet and serve immediately.
