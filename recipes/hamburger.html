Hamburger Recipe

Home

Description

This is a delicious hamburger recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 1 Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties.
  3. Place patties on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on top of each patty and let melt.
  5. Toast the buns on the grill for 30 seconds.
  6. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, and bun.
